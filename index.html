The Citizens' (Owners') Rights Framework

Solution Overview

The controlling framework “Citizens' (Owners') Rights” is a unique and alternative tool for individuals to protect their ownership interests. It offers individual owners – primarily citizens – the chance to protect their ownership interests, potentially much faster than through conventional approaches.

It equips individual owners with a simple, peaceful and non-violent way to assert their rights against organizations they co-own (for example, a citizen is a co-owner of a country).

The solution is designed for individual users; no cooperation or joint effort with other users is required or expected. For individual owners and citizens, the solution is free to use. No software, registration or prior experience is required.

The framework (“The Citizens’ / Owners’ Rights” Framework) is the tool , and its application is the method (“Decentralized Democracy”). It is a controlling solution (not an ideology) that is non-violent and does not include any revolutionary content. Nothing about the framework is extremist.
